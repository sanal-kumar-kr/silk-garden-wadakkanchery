About Silk Garden

For over two decades, Silk Garden has been Wadakkancherry's premier destination for premium textiles and traditional clothing.

We specialize in authentic Kerala sarees, silk fabrics, dress materials, and ready-to-wear ethnic clothing for men, women, and children. Our collection features both traditional handloom products and contemporary designs.
